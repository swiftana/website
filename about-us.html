About Swiftana

Melbourne's trusted cleaning experts delivering premium cleaning services.

Our Service

Swiftana is a customer-friendly cleaning startup offering fast and affordable cleaning solutions for busy households and growing businesses in Melbourne. With an emphasis on convenience and hygiene, Swiftana uses trained staff and quality products to provide hassle-free, high-quality cleaning—swiftly.

We offer residential cleaning including regular, deep, seasonal, and move-out cleans; commercial cleaning for offices, retail spaces, gyms, medical facilities, and construction sites; as well as specialized services such as carpet, upholstery, window cleaning, and eco-friendly options. Custom add-ons include fridge/oven cleans, bed linen changes, and laundry folding.

Our Difference

Founded to meet the growing demand for reliable, professional, and flexible cleaning services that can be booked online and customized easily, Swiftana combines high-quality cleaning with quick and easy access through online bookings, digital invoicing, and tailored cleaning plans. Our approach, fast turnaround, and subscription discounts create a superior customer experience.

Our Clients

Time-poor professionals and families, real estate agencies and property managers, SMEs, co-working spaces, event venues, and environmentally conscious consumers.
